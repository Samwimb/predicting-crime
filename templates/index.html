Washington, DC Crime Predicted

DC crime trends predicted alongside weather predictions and lunar phases

About this Project

The purpose of Project NIGHTLIGHT PREDICTED is the visualization of mutual concurrence of crime, weather, and lunar phases in Washington, DC for the next 5 days. NIGHTLIGHT extracted, transformed, and loaded into SQLAlchemy, daily data from DC Open Data (crime), and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Adminstration (NNDC Climate Online, NASA GSFC and the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbitor [LRO]). Lunar phase data includes the percent of moon illumination seen in the city. We used tensor flow and keras models to make our predictions.
