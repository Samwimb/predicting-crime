Washington, DC Crime Predicted

DC crime trends predicted alongside weather predictions and lunar phases

About this Project

This project, Project Nightlight Predicted, is a continuation of our previous project, Project Nightlight.) The purpose of this project is the visualization of mutual concurrence of crime, weather, and lunar phases in Washington, DC for the next 5-7 days. NIGHTLIGHT looked at daily data from DC Open Data (crime), and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Adminstration (NNDC Climate Online, NASA GSFC and the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbitor [LRO]). Lunar phase data includes the percent of moon illumination seen in the city. We used tensor flow and keras models to make predictions.
